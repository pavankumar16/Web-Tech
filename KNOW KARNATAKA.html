Some facts about the state

->Largest exporter of coffee.

->Flourish most number of tigers.

->Second highest plunge waterfall-Jog falls.

->First to produce rocket artillery in the country.

->Home to first private radio station.

->Only state with permission to make Indian flags.

->Second largest pre-dome-Gol Gumbaz.

->Owns the epitome of Royalty-Mysore Palace.

->Home to land of five rivers-Bijapur.

->Largest walkthrough Aviary.

->Over 13 languages spoken.

->Vijaynagar empire-ruins older than mughal empire.
